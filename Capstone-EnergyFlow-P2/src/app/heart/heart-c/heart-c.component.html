Chakra 4: Heart
















Sanskrit & Meaning: Anahata - “unhurt”, “unstruck”

Location: Center of the rib cage area, near the heart

Element: Air

Sound or Mantra: "Yam"

Color: Green

Message: “I am loving.” “I am an expression of love."

Balanced: Acceptance of self and others, emotionally balanced, feeling interconnected,
friendly, discernment, healthy giving and receiving, compassion

Imbalanced: Dysfunctional relationships; heart, lung, and upper back issues; immune and circulatory
systems issues; heart chakra pain; “savior/rescuer” roles; co-dependence

Ways to Correct Imbalances: Cultivate self care; compassion and love; do healthy activities you love; forgive self
and others; practice gratitude; receive the goodness in life; give good things to others and/or
positive energy (or thoughts) to nature; use the color green in surrounding, nature walks

Chakra 5: Throat













Sanskrit & Meaning: Vishuddha - “purification”, “especially pure”

Location: The middle to base of the throat

Element: Sound, Frequency/Vibration, Space

Sound or Mantra: "Ham"

Color: Blue

Message: “I am speaking truthfully.” “I am expressing my truth.”

Balanced: Healthy communication, ability to create ideas into reality

Imbalanced: Mean/hurtful speech, gossiping, feeling insecure/fear of speaking, inability to
“think before you speak”, lying, shyness, throat issues

Ways to Correct Imbalances: Singing joyfully, speaking your truth, use breathing exercises, chant, use of the color
blue in surroundings, look to the clear skies

Chakra 2: Sacral















Sanskrit & Meaning: Svadhishthana - “One’s own place”, “Your own seat”

Location: Lower abdomen, above the sexual organs and below the navel

Element: Water

Sound or Mantra: "Vam"

Color: Orange

Message: “I am feeling.” “I am the creator of my desires."

Balanced: Ability to fully create desires; curious; optimistic, vibrant, playful,
accurate discernment, trust in “gut feeling”; enthusiastic; connected to
personal development and inner wisdom

Imbalanced: Emotional, sexual and creative imbalances; inability to “feel” or enjoy the surrounding world;
reproductive and lymphatic issues; inflexible; sexual obsessions or lack/repressed sexual desire;
dependencies on others or substances; overly emotional; poor boundaries; feelings of detachment

Ways to Correct Imbalances: Make plans that move you toward your passions; master your sexuality; belly dancing,
stretching; create something (art, music, a written work; use of the color orange in surroundings, visit places with
running water (waterfall, lake, ocean, river, stream, aquarium…)

Chakra 3: Solar Plexus













Sanskrit & Meaning: Manipura - “Jewel City”, “Seat of gems”

Location: upper abdomen between the rib cages

Element: Fire

Sound or Mantra: "Ram"

Color: Yellow to golden yellow

Message: “I am doing.” “I am in control of my own destiny."

Balanced: Intellect, personal power, ability to gain expected results, harmonious with self
and the world, wisdom for personal direction, assertiveness

Imbalanced: Digestive system issues, controlling/manipulative behavior, hopelessness, inability to realize
ideas or dreams, obsessions, no clear direction in life, self-sabotage, fear of change, worry, anger

Ways to Correct Imbalances: Discover what “fires you up” in a healthy way; make changes in your life to discover something you enjoy;
eat a healthy diet; develop your talents and confidence; cultivate optimism; use the color yellow in surroundings;
make plans to accomplish your personal goals and dreams
